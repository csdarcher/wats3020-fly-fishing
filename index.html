Location Hotspot

The Naselle River originates in the Willapa Hills and flows generally west, passing the community of Naselle before emptying into the southern part of Willapa Bay. It is known for its trout and salmon fishing.

About

Andy Darcher grew up in the small town of Naselle located on the southwest coast of Washington State. Living so close to the Columbia River and Willapa Bay, fishing has been a part of his life since as long as he can remember. He started fly fishing around the age of 12 and started tying his own flies soon after. Most days you can find him fly fishing on the Olympic Peninsula for steelhead.
