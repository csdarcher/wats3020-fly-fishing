Featured Fly

This is a Brad's Brat - a classic Steelhead fly designed Enos Bradner.

  • Hook: Salmon/steelhead, No.2/0-6
  • Tag: Flat silver or gold tinsel
  • Tail: Orange pheasant crest
  • Body: Half orange, half red dubbing
  • Rib: Gold or silver oval tinsel
  • Hackle: Brown hen or pheasant
  • Wing: Orange over white polar bear or substitute. For this pattern I substituted a few strands of orange Krystal Flash.
  • Cheeks: Jungle cock (optional)

Location Spotlight

The Naselle is a picturesque river that originates in the Willapa Hills and flows generally west, passing the community of Naselle before emptying into the southern part of Willapa Bay. It is known for its trout and salmon fishing.

About

Andy Darcher grew up in the small town of Naselle located on the southwest coast of Washington State. Living so close to the Columbia River and Willapa Bay, fishing has been a part of his life since as long as he can remember. He started fly fishing around the age of 12 and started tying his own flies soon after. Most days you can find him fly fishing on the Olympic Peninsula for steelhead.
